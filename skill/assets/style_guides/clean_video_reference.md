# Clean Reference Board Prompt Language

This style guide is for generating storyboards intended as direct input (Image-to-Video) for video models.

## Core Directives
- **Write for Image Models:** The prompt must be optimized for AI image generation.
- **Visual Continuity:** Rely entirely on the image to express continuity. Describe the spatial relationships and character consistency precisely.

## Prohibitions (What to Exclude)
To ensure the video model focuses on the visual subject and not artifacts:
- NO large text blocks or paragraphs in the image.
- NO subtitles or captions.
- NO User Interface (UI) overlays.
- NO complex arrows or diagrams.
- NO exaggerated comic book frames or borders.
- NO watermarks or logos (unless it is the product asset itself).

## Emphasis
- Clean, uncluttered visuals.
- Strong silhouettes and clear lighting.
- Predictable and consistent background elements across panels.
