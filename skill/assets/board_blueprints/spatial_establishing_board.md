# Spatial Establishing Board Blueprint (Unified 6-Zone Pitch Sheet)

*This blueprint applies the 6-Zone Unified Storyboard layout to a sequence designed to introduce a location, showing the relationship between different areas (e.g., exterior to interior, macro to micro).*

## Zone 1: Header Row (Metadata)
- **Title/Scene**: Location Reveal [Insert Location, e.g., "The Cyber-Market"]
- **Sequence Type**: Spatial Establishing / World-Building
- **Frame Rate / Runtime**: Base 24fps / Slower pacing
- **Vibe/Tone**: Epic, atmospheric, navigational

## Zone 2: Character & Styling Lock Row
- **Subject**: The Environment itself is the primary character. Secondary characters act as scale reference (e.g., "Tiny silhouettes in the foreground to show massive scale").
- **Architectural Lock**: Define the style (e.g., "Brutalist concrete mixed with neon holograms").
- **Landmarks**: Specific anchor points that appear in multiple shots to prove spatial coherence (e.g., "A giant neon dragon sign").

## Zone 3: Camera & Movement Plan Zone
- **Camera Body/Lens**: Ultra-wide lenses (14mm - 24mm) to capture vast spaces. Drones or crane rigs for high-altitude establishing.
- **Camera Flight Path**: 
  - sweeping, revealing motions.
  - E.g., `Push through the outer gate -> Tilt down to reveal the deep canyon -> Glide forward into the central market stall.`

## Zone 4: Expressions & Action Keyframes Zone
- Map out environmental interactions and scale markers:
  1. *Foreground Element*: A local bird or floating drone passing near the lens to establish depth.
  2. *Scale Marker*: A tiny person walking up huge stairs.
  3. *Threshold detail*: The texture of the door or gate being passed through.

## Zone 5: Core Storyboard Grid (Sequential Panels)
*(Focus on logical spatial relationships. Views must serve understanding without contradictions.)*

- **PANEL 1**: Exterior / Macro View (外部)
  - *ACTION*: Establish the broader geographic or environmental context. The wide establishing shot.
- **PANEL 2**: Entrance / Threshold (入口)
  - *ACTION*: Show the pathway, gate, or transition point into the core space.
- **PANEL 3**: Interior / Wide Layout (内部)
  - *ACTION*: Reveal the overall layout of the specific setting inside the threshold.
- **PANEL 4**: Core Area / Focus Point (核心区域)
  - *ACTION*: Direct attention to the specific location where the main narrative will occur.

## Zone 6: Technical Specs Footer
- **Lighting**: Atmospheric lighting (e.g., god rays, fog, time-of-day specific shadows) to define the volume of the space.
- **Materials/VFX**: Weather effects (rain, dust motes), large-scale atmospheric perspective (distant objects fade into fog).
- **Color Script**: Establish the dominant color palette of the world (e.g., toxic yellows and deep shadows).
