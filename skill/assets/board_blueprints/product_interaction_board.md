# Product / Prop Interaction Board Blueprint (Unified 6-Zone Pitch Sheet)

*This blueprint applies the 6-Zone Unified Storyboard layout to a sequence focused on a character interacting with a specific product or prop, ensuring the object is highlighted correctly and consistently.*

## Zone 1: Header Row (Metadata)
- **Title/Scene**: Product Feature [Insert Product, e.g., "Smartwatch Interface Use"]
- **Sequence Type**: Product/Prop Interaction
- **Frame Rate / Runtime**: Base 30fps (often used for commercial)
- **Vibe/Tone**: Commercial, polished, clear, tactile

## Zone 2: Character & Styling Lock Row
- **Subject**: Focus heavily on the interacting limbs (e.g., "Manicured hands, neutral skin tone, rolled-up sleeves to avoid distracting from the product").
- **Product Lock**: Absolute rigid definition of the product (e.g., "Matte black smartwatch, silver bezel, glowing green UI").
- **Scale/Proportion Lock**: Define the exact size of the product relative to the human hand.

## Zone 3: Camera & Movement Plan Zone
- **Camera Body/Lens**: High-fidelity commercial lenses (e.g., 100mm Macro) for extreme sharpness and zero distortion.
- **Camera Flight Path**: 
  - Smooth, elegant movements.
  - E.g., `Slow pan along the edge of the product -> Rack focus from the user's eye to the product in their hand.`

## Zone 4: Expressions & Action Keyframes Zone
- Highlight the exact mechanics of the interaction:
  1. *The Grip*: How the fingers hold the object (pinch, palm, wrap).
  2. *The Actuation*: A finger pressing a button or swiping a screen.
  3. *The Feedback*: The product lighting up, opening, or responding.
  4. *User Reaction*: A subtle smile or nod of satisfaction.

## Zone 5: Core Storyboard Grid (Sequential Panels)
*(Focus on scale, visibility, and un-obscured angles of the product)*

- **PANEL 1**: Context / Introduction (引入)
  - *ACTION*: Establish the character and the product/prop in the same space.
- **PANEL 2**: Approach / Reach (接近)
  - *ACTION*: Show the character's intent toward the object. Focus on the distance closing.
- **PANEL 3**: Hand Interaction Point (接触点)
  - *ACTION*: Clearly display how the character physically engages with the object. Do NOT hide the product behind the hand.
- **PANEL 4**: Feature Demonstration (特征展示)
  - *ACTION*: Highlight the product/prop in use. Insert macro shot.
- **PANEL 5**: Final Display State (最终展示)
  - *ACTION*: The "hero shot" showing the product/prop clearly, often with the character's reaction.

## Zone 6: Technical Specs Footer
- **Lighting**: Commercial product lighting. Multiple soft sources to create elegant reflections and edge-highlights on the product materials (e.g., glass, metal).
- **Materials/VFX**: High gloss on screens, brushed metal textures, UI elements glowing with accurate luminance.
- **Color Script**: Brand-compliant color palette. The background should contrast with the product to make it pop.
